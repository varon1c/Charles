ABOUT US
Welcome to MJV Van Transportation, your trusted partner for van rentals in Pampanga since 2016. Whether you’re traveling for business, leisure, or family occasions, MJV is committed to providing safe, reliable, and comfortable transport solutions that cater to all your needs.

Who We Are

MJV Van Transportation was founded in 2016 with a simple goal: to make van rentals hassle-free and accessible for everyone in Pampanga and beyond. Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the most trusted providers of van transport, offering top-quality service with our fleet of well-maintained vans.

Our Services

  • Airport Transfers - Convenient and timely transportation to and from major airports.
  • Group Outings - Ideal for family getaways, team-building activities, and group tours.
  • Event Transport - Reliable transport for weddings, conferences, and other special events.
  • Customizable Trips - Tailor-made travel plans to fit your specific needs, whether it’s a day trip or an extended journey.

Why Choose MJV?

  • Experienced Drivers - Our professional drivers are highly experienced and prioritize your safety and comfort.
  • Well-Maintained Vans - We take pride in our fleet, ensuring every vehicle is clean, well-maintained, and equipped with modern amenities.
  • Affordable Rates - We offer competitive pricing to suit different budgets without compromising on quality.
